LIVE PERFORMANCE

A live performance refers to a presentation, show, or entertainment event in which the performers or participants interact with the audience in real-time, typically in a physical or virtual setting. Live performances can take various forms, including music concerts, theater productions, dance shows, stand-up comedy, speeches, sports events, and more. The defining characteristic of a live performance is that it occurs "live," meaning it happens in the immediate presence of the audience and is not pre-recorded or edited.
